Patented Technology

Brain.fm holds patents on key processes for creating functional music, including technology to elicit string neural phase locking -allowing popularity of neurons to engage in verious kinds of coordinated activity-and technology to remove distractions in sound.

This makes our music unique, purpose-built to steer you into a desired mental state. In other words, we've found new ways to create music that helps you do what you need to do.

Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientists at Brain.fm work with collacborators at academic institutions to run experiments.

We look at the effects of our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests, measuring performance on simpla games.

Our experiments always include a control condition ('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes the difference.
